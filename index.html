This Privacy Policy explains how Leagun Technologies LLC collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Information We Collect

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about our services via SMS, email, and phone

SMS Consent & Privacy

Your phone number and SMS consent are collected when you submit a form on our website. SMS opt-in data and consent are used solely for communications from Leagun Technologies LLC and are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in information is never shared with anyone for promotional reasons.

No Sharing of SMS Opt-In Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent with any third parties, affiliates, or marketing partners. Your SMS consent data is used exclusively by Leagun Technologies LLC to communicate with you directly.

User Rights

  • You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
  • You may unsubscribe from emails using the link in any email or by contacting us directly
  • You may request that we update or delete your personal information by contacting us at contact@leagun.com

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is fully secure.
